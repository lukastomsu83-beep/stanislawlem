I read a lot but never write anything down. After finishing a book I'd find that within a few months, the details — the specific things that made it good or bad — were gone. All that was left was a vague sense of "I liked it" or "it was fine."

This site is a fix for that. A place to keep track of what I've read, what I thought, and occasionally write something longer when a book really sticks.

It's also an excuse to learn Hugo, which has been on my list for a while.

## What you'll find here

- **Books** — short entries on everything I read, with a few sentences on what worked and what didn't
- **Posts** — longer pieces when I have something more to say

Nothing fancy. Just notes, in public.
